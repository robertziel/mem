### Meta, Apple & Netflix Behavioral Interviews

**Meta (Facebook) core values:**
- **Move Fast** — bias toward action, iterative delivery
- **Be Bold** — take risks, innovate, challenge the status quo
- **Be Open** — transparent communication, share information freely
- **Build Social Value** — impact on people and communities
- **Focus on Long-Term Impact** — sustainable, not just quick wins

**Meta behavioral focus areas:**
- Initiative: "Tell me about something you built that wasn't asked of you."
- Speed: "Describe a time you shipped something quickly. What tradeoffs did you make?"
- Openness: "Tell me about a time you gave or received tough feedback."
- Failure recovery: "Describe a time you failed. How did you recover?"
- Impact at scale: "What's the most impactful project you've worked on? How did you measure impact?"

**Apple core values:**
- **Attention to detail / Craftsmanship** — obsess over quality and user experience
- **Cross-functional collaboration** — work closely with design, hardware, marketing
- **Secrecy and focus** — work within constraints, prioritize ruthlessly
- **Perseverance** — push through obstacles to deliver excellence
- **Innovation** — creative problem-solving, thinking differently

**Apple behavioral focus areas:**
- Craftsmanship: "Tell me about a time you went beyond the requirements to deliver something exceptional."
- Collaboration: "Describe working with a non-engineering team (design, PM)."
- Conflict: "How do you handle disagreements about product direction?"
- Detail: "Tell me about a bug you found that others missed."
- Resilience: "Describe a time a project was almost cancelled. What did you do?"

**Netflix culture (Freedom & Responsibility):**
- **Judgment** — make wise decisions despite ambiguity
- **Communication** — listen well, be concise, treat people with respect
- **Curiosity** — learn rapidly, seek to understand strategy and market
- **Courage** — say what you think even if it's controversial, question actions inconsistent with values
- **Selflessness** — seek what's best for Netflix, not yourself or your team
- **Innovation** — re-conceptualize issues to discover solutions, challenge prevailing assumptions

**Netflix behavioral focus areas:**
- Independent judgment: "Tell me about a time you made a decision without your manager."
- Candor: "Describe a time you gave feedback that was uncomfortable."
- Courage: "Tell me about a time you challenged a popular decision."
- Context over control: "How do you ensure alignment without micromanaging?"
- Talent density: "How do you handle working with someone who's underperforming?"

**Cross-company preparation strategy:**
1. Map your stories to each company's specific values BEFORE the interview
2. Use the same story bank but emphasize different aspects:
   - Meta: emphasize speed and boldness
   - Apple: emphasize craftsmanship and detail
   - Netflix: emphasize independent judgment and candor
   - Amazon: emphasize ownership and data-driven decisions
   - Google: emphasize collaboration and intellectual humility

**Rule of thumb:** Research the specific company's values before your interview. Tailor your story emphasis to match. The same conflict resolution story can highlight "Move Fast" (Meta), "Courage" (Netflix), or "Disagree and Commit" (Amazon) depending on which aspect you emphasize.
