### Amazon Leadership Principles Interview

**How Amazon uses LPs:**
- Every interview round includes LP behavioral questions
- Even coding rounds have LP questions woven in
- Interviewers are assigned specific LPs to assess
- Bar raiser evaluates overall LP strength

**16 Leadership Principles (most tested in bold):**

| # | Principle | What they assess |
|---|-----------|-----------------|
| 1 | **Customer Obsession** | Start with customer, work backwards |
| 2 | **Ownership** | Think long-term, act on behalf of entire company |
| 3 | Invent and Simplify | Innovate, find simpler solutions |
| 4 | Are Right, A Lot | Good judgment, seek diverse perspectives |
| 5 | Learn and Be Curious | Never stop learning, explore new possibilities |
| 6 | **Hire and Develop the Best** | Raise the bar, mentor others |
| 7 | Insist on the Highest Standards | Relentlessly high standards |
| 8 | Think Big | Bold direction, think differently |
| 9 | Bias for Action | Speed matters, calculated risk-taking |
| 10 | Frugality | Do more with less |
| 11 | Earn Trust | Listen, speak candidly, self-critical |
| 12 | **Dive Deep** | Stay connected to details, audit, verify data |
| 13 | **Have Backbone; Disagree and Commit** | Challenge, then commit fully |
| 14 | **Deliver Results** | Focus on key inputs, deliver with quality |
| 15 | Strive to be Earth's Best Employer | Work environment, empathy |
| 16 | Success and Scale Bring Broad Responsibility | Better every day |

**Most common LP questions by principle:**

**Customer Obsession:**
- "Tell me about a time you went above and beyond for a customer."
- "Describe when you had to make a trade-off between customer needs and business goals."

**Ownership:**
- "Tell me about a time you took on something outside your responsibility."
- "Describe a project where you saw it through from start to finish."

**Dive Deep:**
- "Tell me about a time you had to dig into data to solve a problem."
- "Describe when you found an error that others missed."

**Have Backbone; Disagree and Commit:**
- "Tell me about a time you disagreed with your manager."
- "Describe when you had to push back on a popular decision."

**Deliver Results:**
- "Tell me about a time you delivered a project under tight deadlines."
- "Describe when you had to overcome significant obstacles to hit a goal."

**Scoring:**
- Each LP is rated: Strong Hire / Hire / No Hire / Strong No Hire
- Need "Hire" or better on most LPs
- One "Strong No Hire" from bar raiser typically blocks the offer

**Rule of thumb:** Prepare 2 stories per LP you expect to be tested on. Use data and metrics in every answer. Show ownership by saying "I" not "we." Amazon wants specifics: what you did, why, and exact results.
