### Netflix Behavioral Interview

**Netflix culture values (Freedom & Responsibility):**
- **Judgment** — make wise decisions despite ambiguity, use data
- **Communication** — listen well, be concise, treat people with respect
- **Curiosity** — learn rapidly, seek to understand strategy and market
- **Courage** — say what you think even if controversial, question inconsistencies
- **Selflessness** — seek what's best for Netflix, not yourself or your team
- **Innovation** — re-conceptualize issues, challenge prevailing assumptions

**What Netflix evaluates:**
- Independent decision-making (can you act without your manager?)
- Radical candor (will you give honest, direct feedback?)
- Talent density mindset (do you raise the bar for the team?)
- Context over control (do you understand WHY, not just WHAT?)
- Impact and ownership (do you drive results end-to-end?)

**Common Netflix behavioral questions:**

**Judgment / Independent Decision:**
- "Tell me about a time you made a significant decision without your manager's input."
- "Describe a situation where you had to choose between two good options with limited data."
- "How do you decide what to work on when priorities aren't clear?"

**Courage / Candor:**
- "Describe a time you gave feedback that was uncomfortable to deliver."
- "Tell me about a time you challenged a popular decision or consensus."
- "How do you handle disagreements with senior leadership?"

**Selflessness:**
- "Tell me about a time you made a decision that was best for the company but not for your team."
- "Describe when you helped another team succeed at the expense of your own timeline."

**Context over Control:**
- "How do you ensure alignment without micromanaging?"
- "Describe how you've set context for your team to make good decisions independently."

**Talent Density:**
- "How do you handle working with someone who's underperforming?"
- "Tell me about a time you raised the performance bar for your team."
- "How do you attract and retain top talent?"

**Innovation:**
- "Tell me about a time you fundamentally rethought how something was done."
- "Describe a time you challenged a 'we've always done it this way' approach."

**How to tailor your stories for Netflix:**
- Emphasize INDEPENDENT JUDGMENT: "I assessed the situation and made the call"
- Emphasize CANDOR: "I told my manager directly that the approach was wrong, with data"
- Emphasize COMPANY OVER TEAM: "It cost my team a sprint, but it was right for Netflix"
- Emphasize CONTEXT: "I documented the strategy so the team could make decisions without me"
- No "I asked my manager" stories — Netflix wants autonomous decision-makers

**Rule of thumb:** Netflix values people who can operate independently with good judgment. Show courage (challenge the status quo), candor (give hard feedback directly), and selflessness (company over team). Every answer should show you make decisions, not wait for direction. "I decided" beats "I escalated" every time.
