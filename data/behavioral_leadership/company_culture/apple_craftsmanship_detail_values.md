### Apple Behavioral Interview

**Apple core values:**
- **Attention to Detail / Craftsmanship** — obsess over quality and user experience
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration** — work closely with design, hardware, marketing
- **Secrecy and Focus** — work within constraints, prioritize ruthlessly
- **Perseverance** — push through obstacles to deliver excellence
- **Innovation** — creative problem-solving, thinking differently

**What Apple evaluates:**
- Quality over speed (did you ship something excellent, not just fast?)
- Collaboration across disciplines (can you work with designers, PMs, hardware?)
- Attention to detail (do you notice things others miss?)
- Resilience (did you push through when things got hard?)
- Creative problem-solving (did you find an elegant solution?)

**Common Apple behavioral questions:**

**Craftsmanship / Detail:**
- "Tell me about a time you went beyond the requirements to deliver something exceptional."
- "Describe a bug or issue you found that others missed. How did you catch it?"
- "Tell me about a time you pushed back on a design because it wasn't good enough."

**Collaboration:**
- "Describe a time you worked closely with a non-engineering team (design, PM, QA)."
- "How do you handle disagreements about product direction?"
- "Tell me about a project where cross-team coordination was critical."

**Perseverance / Resilience:**
- "Describe a time a project was almost cancelled. What did you do?"
- "Tell me about the hardest technical problem you've solved."
- "Describe a time you had to deliver under extremely tight constraints."

**Innovation:**
- "Tell me about a creative solution you came up with for a technical problem."
- "Describe a time you simplified a complex system or process."

**How to tailor your stories for Apple:**
- Emphasize QUALITY: "I spent an extra day polishing the edge cases because users would notice"
- Emphasize DETAIL: "I noticed a 2px misalignment in the UI that the designer had missed"
- Emphasize CRAFT: "I refactored the code to be elegant, not just working"
- Emphasize COLLABORATION: "I worked closely with the design team to get the interaction exactly right"
- Emphasize USER EXPERIENCE: "I thought about how this would feel to the end user"

**Rule of thumb:** Apple values craftsmanship over speed. Show you care deeply about quality and user experience. Demonstrate attention to detail. Show you can collaborate with designers and non-engineers. "I took extra time to make it excellent" is the right answer at Apple (unlike Meta where speed wins).
