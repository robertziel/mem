### Meta (Facebook) Behavioral Interview

**Meta core values:**
- **Move Fast** — bias toward action, iterative delivery, ship and learn
- **Be Bold** — take risks, innovate, challenge the status quo
- **Be Open** — transparent communication, share information freely
- **Build Social Value** — impact on people and communities
- **Focus on Long-Term Impact** — sustainable decisions, not just quick wins

**What Meta evaluates:**
- Initiative and ownership (did you do it without being asked?)
- Speed of execution (how fast did you ship?)
- Learning from failure (did you recover quickly?)
- Impact at scale (measurable results, millions of users)
- Open communication (giving and receiving tough feedback)

**Common Meta behavioral questions:**

**Initiative / Move Fast:**
- "Tell me about something you built that wasn't asked of you."
- "Describe a time you shipped something quickly. What tradeoffs did you make?"
- "Tell me about a time you identified a problem and fixed it proactively."

**Boldness / Risk:**
- "Describe a time you took a risk on a technical decision. How did it turn out?"
- "Tell me about a time you challenged a process or approach at your company."

**Openness / Feedback:**
- "Tell me about a time you gave tough feedback to a colleague."
- "Describe a time you received critical feedback. How did you respond?"
- "How do you ensure knowledge sharing on your team?"

**Failure / Recovery:**
- "Describe a time you failed. How did you recover?"
- "Tell me about a project that didn't go as planned. What did you learn?"

**Impact:**
- "What's the most impactful project you've worked on? How did you measure impact?"
- "Describe a time you improved a system that affected many users."

**How to tailor your stories for Meta:**
- Emphasize SPEED: "I shipped the first version in 3 days, iterated based on feedback"
- Emphasize INITIATIVE: "I noticed the problem and proposed a fix without being asked"
- Emphasize MEASURABLE IMPACT: "Reduced latency by 40%, impacting 2M daily users"
- Emphasize LEARNING: "The first approach failed, but I pivoted in a week and succeeded"

**Rule of thumb:** Meta values builders who move fast, take initiative, and measure impact. Show you can ship quickly AND learn from mistakes. Every story should have a quantified result. Emphasize speed and boldness — "I shipped first, then iterated" beats "I planned for months."
